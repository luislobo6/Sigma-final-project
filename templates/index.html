Mexico Banking Industry



Welcome to the Mexican Banking industry dashboard. We hope to give you a brief recap of how the industry has evolved throughout the years and see the impacts of the latest macroeconomic conditions on the sector finalizing with a projection of what to expect.


Mexico has one of the most developed banking system, more than 60% of banking assets belong to international banks where spaniards BBVA and Santander lead the industry along with Citibanamex and local mexico bank Banorte.


For easiness we have created a menu in which you can select the visualization you want to cover, from benchmark performance, portfolio historic and projected trends and 4Q financial results on the four main banks.


For projections we used the newly developed Facebook Prophet application, that help us create a time series analysis model that we trained with data until 2018. We tested with 2019 and 2020 and reviewed projections for 2021 onwards.


We expect to have provided enough insight on the Mexican Banking System, for final overview please read our conclusions.
